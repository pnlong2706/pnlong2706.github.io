ABOUT ME


Hello, I'm Long


I am Pham Ngoc Long, from a coastal village in Hue, and I am currently a second-year student at Ho Chi Minh University of Technology. My passion for math has blossomed since I was a child. From elementary school to high school, I enjoyed exploring interesting math problems and participating in some local math competitions. Therefore, I decided to attend Quoc Hoc Hue High School for the Gifted. After that, I successfully completed the graduation exam with a score of 29.15 and chose the computer science major at Ho Chi Minh University of Technology.


I am a person who likes to discover new knowledge, especially in the field of programming. Always be serious about work and study, and strive to become a good programmer in the future. Besides studying, my hobbies are reading books and watching movies.
